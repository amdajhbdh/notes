# Satellites - Energy

> Energy considerations in orbital motion

## Key Concepts
1. Kinetic Energy in Orbit
2. Potential Energy in Orbit
3. Total Mechanical Energy
4. Escape Velocity

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Orbital KE**: $E_k = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- **Gravitational PE**: $E_p = -\frac{GMm}{r}$
- **Total Energy**: $E = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- **Escape Velocity**: $v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{r}}$
