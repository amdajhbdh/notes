# Satellites - Gravitational Force

> Universal gravitation and satellite motion

## Key Concepts
1. Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation
2. Gravitational Field
3. Weightlessness
4. Gravitational Potential

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Gravitational Force**: $F = \frac{Gm_1m_2}{r^2}$
- **Gravitational Field**: $g = \frac{GM}{r^2}$
- **Gravitational Potential**: $V = -\frac{GM}{r}$
