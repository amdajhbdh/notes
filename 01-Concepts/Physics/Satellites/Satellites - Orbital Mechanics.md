# Satellites - Orbital Mechanics

> Motion of artificial and natural satellites

## Key Concepts
1. Orbital Motion
2. Centripetal Force
3. Orbital Velocity
4. Orbital Period

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Orbital Velocity**: $v = \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}$
- **Orbital Period**: $T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{r^3}{GM}}$
- **Centripetal Acceleration**: $a_c = \frac{v^2}{r} = \frac{GM}{r^2}$
