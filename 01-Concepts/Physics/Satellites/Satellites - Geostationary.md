# Satellites - Geostationary

> Special orbit for communications satellites

## Key Concepts
1. Geostationary Orbit Definition
2. Orbital Period (24 hours)
3. Altitude (~35,786 km)
4. Applications

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Geostationary Radius**: $r = \left(\frac{GMT^2}{4\pi^2}\right)^{1/3}$
- **Altitude**: $h = r - R_E$
