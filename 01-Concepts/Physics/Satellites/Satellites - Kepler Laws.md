# Satellites - Kepler Laws

> Empirical laws describing planetary motion

## Key Concepts
1. First Law (Elliptical Orbits)
2. Second Law (Equal Areas)
3. Third Law (Period-Distance Relation)
4. Application to Satellites

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Third Law**: $T^2 = \frac{4\pi^2}{GM}r^3$
- **Area Rate**: $\frac{dA}{dt} = \text{constant}$
