# EM - Lorentz Force

> Combined electric and magnetic force on charged particles

## Key Concepts
1. Electric Force on Charge
2. Magnetic Force on Moving Charge
3. Cross Product Direction
4. Particle Accelerators

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Lorentz Force**: $\vec{F} = q(\vec{E} + \vec{v} \times \vec{B})$
- **Magnetic Force**: $F = qvB\sin\theta$
