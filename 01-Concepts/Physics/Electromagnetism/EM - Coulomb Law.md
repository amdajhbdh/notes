## Mathematical Formulas
- **Coulomb's Law**: $ F = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{|q_1 q_2|}{r^2} $
- **Electric Field**: $ \vec{E} = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q \vec{r}}{r^3} $
