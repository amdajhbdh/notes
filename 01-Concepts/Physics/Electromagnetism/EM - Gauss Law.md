# EM - Gauss Law

> Relationship between electric fields and charge distribution

## Key Concepts
1. Electric Flux
2. Gaussian Surface
3. Enclosed Charge
4. Symmetry

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Gauss's Law**: $\oint \vec{E} \cdot \vec{dA} = \frac{Q_{enc}}{\epsilon_0}$
- **Electric Flux**: $\Phi_E = \vec{E} \cdot \vec{A}$
