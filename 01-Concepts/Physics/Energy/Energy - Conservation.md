# Energy - Conservation

> Principle of energy conservation in isolated systems

## Key Concepts
1. Isolated Systems
2. Total Mechanical Energy
3. Energy Transformation
4. Non-conservative Forces

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Total Energy**: $E_{total} = E_k + E_p = \text{constant}$
- **With friction**: $E_{final} = E_{initial} - W_{friction}$
