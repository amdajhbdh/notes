# Energy - Power

> Rate of energy transfer or conversion

## Key Concepts
1. Average Power
2. Instantaneous Power
3. Horsepower
4. Efficiency

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Average Power**: $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{\Delta E}{t}$
- **Instantaneous Power**: $P = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{v}$
- **Efficiency**: $\eta = \frac{P_{useful}}{P_{total}} \times 100\%$
