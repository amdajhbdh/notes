# Energy - Work-Energy Theorem

> Relationship between work and kinetic energy

## Key Concepts
1. Work as Energy Transfer
2. Net Work
3. Kinetic Energy Theorem
4. Work done by various forces

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Work**: $W = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{d} = Fd\cos\theta$
- **Work-Energy Theorem**: $W_{net} = \Delta E_k$
