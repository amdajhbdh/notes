# Circuits - Power

> Electrical power in DC and AC circuits

## Key Concepts
1. DC Power
2. AC Power
3. Power Factor
4. Apparent vs Real Power

## Mathematical Formulas
- **DC Power**: $P = VI = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R}$
- **AC Power**: $P = V_{rms}I_{rms}\cos\phi$
- **Power Factor**: $\cos\phi = \frac{R}{Z}$
