# Circuits - AC vs DC

> Alternating current and direct current characteristics

## Key Concepts
1. Direct Current (DC)
2. Alternating Current (AC)
3. RMS Values
4. Frequency
5. Phase

## Mathematical Formulas
- **DC Voltage**: $V = \text{constant}$
- **AC Voltage**: $V = V_0\sin(\omega t)$
- **RMS Voltage**: $V_{rms} = \frac{V_0}{\sqrt{2}}$
