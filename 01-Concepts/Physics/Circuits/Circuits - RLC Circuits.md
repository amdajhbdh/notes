# Circuits - RLC Circuits

> Resistor-inductor-capacitor circuits

## Key Concepts
1. Series RLC
2. Parallel RLC
3. Resonance
4. Impedance

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Impedance**: $Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$
- **Resonant Frequency**: $\omega_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}$
- **Reactance**: $X_L = \omega L$, $X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C}$
