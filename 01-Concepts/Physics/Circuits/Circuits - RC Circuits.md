# Circuits - RC Circuits

> Resistor-capacitor circuits and charging/discharging

## Key Concepts
1. Capacitor Charging
2. Capacitor Discharging
3. Time Constant
4. Exponential Behavior

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Charging**: $V(t) = V_0(1 - e^{-t/RC})$
- **Discharging**: $V(t) = V_0e^{-t/RC}$
- **Time Constant**: $\tau = RC$
