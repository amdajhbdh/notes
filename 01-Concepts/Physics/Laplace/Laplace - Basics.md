# Laplace - Basics

> Force on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field

## Key Concepts
1. Laplace Force Formula
2. Direction (Right-hand Rule)
3. Magnetic Field B
4. Current I
5. Conductor Length L

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Laplace Force**: $\vec{F} = I\vec{L} \times \vec{B}$
- **Magnitude**: $F = ILB\sin\theta$
