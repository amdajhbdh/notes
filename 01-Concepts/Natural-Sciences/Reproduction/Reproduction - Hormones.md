# Reproduction - Hormones

> Hormonal regulation of reproduction

## Key Concepts
1. Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Gonadal Axis
2. Gonadotropins
3. Sex Steroids
4. Feedback Mechanisms

## Hormones

### Hypothalamus
- **GnRH**: Releases LH and FSH

### Anterior Pituitary
- **FSH** (Follicle Stimulating Hormone)
  - Females: Follicle development
  - Males: Spermatogenesis
- **LH** (Luteinizing Hormone)
  - Females: Ovulation, corpus luteum
  - Males: Testosterone production

### Gonads
- **Estrogen** (from ovaries)
  - Female secondary characteristics
  - Endometrium proliferation
  - Feedback regulation
  
- **Progesterone** (from corpus luteum)
  - Endometrium maintenance
  - Pregnancy support
  - Negative feedback

- **Testosterone** (from testes)
  - Male secondary characteristics
  - Spermatogenesis

## Feedback Loops

### Female (Menstrual Cycle)
- High estrogen → Positive feedback → LH surge
- Progesterone → Negative feedback

### Male
- Testosterone → Negative feedback on LH/FSH
