# Reproduction - Female System

> Female reproductive anatomy and function

## Key Concepts
1. Ovaries
2. Oogenesis
3. Fallopian Tubes
4. Uterus
5. Menstrual Cycle
6. Ovulation

## Anatomy

### Primary Organs
- **Ovaries**: Produce oocytes and hormones
- **Fallopian Tubes**: Site of fertilization
- **Uterus**: Site of implantation
- **Cervix**: Gateway to uterus
- **Vagina**: Birth canal

## Oogenesis
- Primary oocytes (2n)
- Arrested in prophase I
- Completes at ovulation
- Second division at fertilization

## Menstrual Cycle (~28 days)

### Follicular Phase (Days 1-14)
- FSH stimulates follicle growth
- Estrogen rises
- Endometrium proliferates

### Ovulation (Day 14)
- LH surge
- Egg released

### Luteal Phase (Days 15-28)
- Corpus luteum forms
- Progesterone dominates
- Endometrium secretory
