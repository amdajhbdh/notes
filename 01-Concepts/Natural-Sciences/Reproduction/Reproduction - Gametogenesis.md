# Reproduction - Gametogenesis

> Formation of gametes (sperm and eggs)

## Key Concepts
1. Meiosis Overview
2. Spermatogenesis
3. Oogenesis
4. Comparison of Processes

## Spermatogenesis

### Location
- Seminiferous tubules (testes)

### Process
1. Spermatogonia (2n) → Primary spermatocytes (2n)
2. Meiosis I → Secondary spermatocytes (n)
3. Meiosis II → Spermatids (n)
4. Spermiogenesis → Spermatozoa (n)

### Products
- 4 haploid sperm per primary spermatocyte
- Continuous from puberty

## Oogenesis

### Location
- Ovary

### Process
1. Oogonia (2n) → Primary oocytes (2n) [arrested in prophase I]
2. Ovulation → Secondary oocyte (n) [arrested in metaphase II]
3. Fertilization → Ovum (n)

### Products
- 1 ovum + polar bodies
- Cyclic, from puberty to menopause

## Comparison
| Feature | Spermatogenesis | Oogenesis |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Location | Testes | Ovaries |
| Products | 4 sperm | 1 ovum |
| Timing | Continuous | Cyclic |
| Completion | Meiosis at formation | Meiosis at fertilization |
