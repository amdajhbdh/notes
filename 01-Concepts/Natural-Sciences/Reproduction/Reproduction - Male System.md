# Reproduction - Male System

> Male reproductive anatomy and function

## Key Concepts
1. Testes
2. Spermatogenesis
3. Epididymis
4. Vas Deferens
5. Accessory Glands
6. Semen Composition

## Anatomy

### Primary Organs
- **Testes**: Produce sperm and testosterone
- **Epididymis**: Sperm maturation
- **Vas Deferens**: Sperm transport

### Accessory Glands
- **Seminal Vesicles**: Fructose-rich fluid
- **Prostate**: Alkaline fluid
- **Bulbourethral**: Pre-ejaculate

## Spermatogenesis
- Continuous process
- ~100 million sperm/day
- Takes ~74 days

## Hormonal Control
- **FSH**: Spermatogenesis
- **LH**: Testosterone production
- **Testosterone**: Male characteristics
