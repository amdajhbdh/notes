# Reproduction - Development

> From zygto to embryo

## Key Concepts
1. Cleavage
2. Morula
3. Blastula/Blastocyst
4. Gastrulation
5. Organogenesis

## Development Stages

### Week 1
- **Zygote**: Single cell
- **Cleavage**: Mitotic divisions
- **Morula**: 16-cell ball
- **Blastocyst**: Hollow sphere with ICM

### Week 2
- **Implantation**: In uterine wall
- **Gastrulation begins**
- **Yolk sac**, **Amnion** form

### Week 3
- **Gastrulation**: Three germ layers
- **Neural tube** forms
- **Primitive streak**

### Weeks 4-8 (Embryonic)
- **Organogenesis**
- **Heart beating**
- **Limb buds**
- **All major organs form**

### Week 9+ (Fetal)
- **Rapid growth**
- **Tissues differentiate**
- **Refinement**

## Germ Layers

| Layer | Derivatives |
|-------|-------------|
| Ectoderm | Skin, nervous system, sense organs |
| Mesoderm | Muscles, bones, circulatory system |
| Endoderm | Digestive tract, lungs, liver |
