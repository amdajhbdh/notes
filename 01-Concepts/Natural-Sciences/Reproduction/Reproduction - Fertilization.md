# Reproduction - Fertilization

> Union of sperm and egg

## Key Concepts
1. Sperm Capacitation
2. Acrosome Reaction
3. Membrane Fusion
4. Block to Polyspermy
5. Zygote Formation

## Process Steps

### 1. Capacitation
- Sperm maturation in female tract
- Membrane changes
- Hyperactivity

### 2. Acrosome Reaction
- Enzymes released
- Zona pellucida penetration
- Species specificity

### 3. Membrane Fusion
- Sperm head enters egg
- Cortical reaction begins

### 4. Block to Polyspermy
- Fast block: membrane depolarization
- Slow block: cortical granules

### 5. Activation
- Metabolic activation
- DNA synthesis begins
- Mitosis准备工作

## Result
- **Zygote**: Diploid cell (2n)
- **Genetic uniqueness determined**
- **Sex determined** (X or Y from sperm)
