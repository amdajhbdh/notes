# Geology - Rock Types

> Classification of rocks

## Key Concepts
1. Igneous
2. Sedimentary
3. Metamorphic
4. Rock Cycle
5. Formation Processes

## Rock Types

### Igneous
- **Formation**: Cooling of magma/lava
- **Examples**: Granite, Basalt, Obsidian
- **Texture**: Based on cooling rate

### Sedimentary
- **Formation**: Compaction and cementation
- **Types**: Clastic, Chemical, Organic
- **Examples**: Sandstone, Limestone, Coal

### Metamorphic
- **Formation**: Heat and pressure
- **Types**: Foliated, Non-foliated
- **Examples**: Marble, Slate, Gneiss

## Rock Cycle
Magma → Igneous → Weathering → Sediment → Sedimentary → Heat/Pressure → Metamorphic → Melting → Magma

## Mauritania Context
- Extensive Precambrian basement
- Sedimentary basins
- Some metamorphic units
