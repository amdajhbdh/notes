# Genetics - Transcription

> DNA to RNA synthesis

## Key Concepts
1. Transcription Overview
2. Promoter Regions
3. RNA Polymerase
4. Elongation
5. Termination

## Process Steps

### 1. Initiation
- RNA polymerase binds to promoter
- DNA unwinds at initiation site

### 2. Elongation
- RNA polymerase reads template strand
- Complementary RNA nucleotides added
- 5' to 3' direction

### 3. Termination
- Terminator sequence reached
- RNA polymerase releases
- Pre-mRNA produced

## Types of RNA
- **mRNA**: Carries genetic code to ribosomes
- **tRNA**: Transfers amino acids
- **rRNA**: Ribosomal RNA (structural)
