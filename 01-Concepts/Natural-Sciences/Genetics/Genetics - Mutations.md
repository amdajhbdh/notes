# Genetics - Mutations

> Changes in DNA sequence

1. Mutation Types
2. Point Mutations
3. Frameshift Mutations
4. Chromosomal Mutations
5. Mutagens

## Mutation Classification

### Point Mutations
- **Substitution**: One base replaced
  - Silent: same amino acid
  - Missense: different amino acid
  - Nonsense: stop codon

### Frameshift Mutations
- Insertion or deletion
- Shifts reading frame
- Often severe effects

## Chromosomal Mutations
- Deletions
- Duplications
- Inversions
- Translocations

## Mutagens
- Radiation (UV, X-rays)
- Chemicals
- Viruses
