# Nervous - CNS

> Central nervous system structure

## Key Concepts
1. Brain
2. Spinal Cord
3. Protection
4. Integration
5. Control Centers

## Brain Regions

### Forebrain
- **Cerebrum**: Higher functions
  - Frontal: Planning, reasoning
  - Parietal: Sensory
  - Temporal: Memory, hearing
  - Occipital: Vision
- **Diencephalon**
  - Thalamus: Relay
  - Hypothalamus: Homeostasis

### Midbrain
- Vision, hearing
- Motor control

### Hindbrain
- **Cerebellum**: Coordination
- **Medulla**: Vital functions
- **Pons**: Bridge, breathing

## Spinal Cord
- Relay pathway
- Reflex arcs
- Gray matter (inner)
- White matter (outer)

## Protection
- Skull (cranium)
- Meninges (3 layers)
- CSF (cerebrospinal fluid)
