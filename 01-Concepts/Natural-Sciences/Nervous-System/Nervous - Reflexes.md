# Nervous - Reflexes

> Automatic responses to stimuli

## Key Concepts
1. Reflex Arc
2. Spinal Reflexes
3. Cranial Reflexes
4. Conditioning
5. Reflex Hierarchy

## Reflex Arc Components

1. **Receptor**: Detects stimulus
2. **Sensory Neuron**: Carries to CNS
3. **Integration Center**: Synapse in spinal cord/brain
4. **Motor Neuron**: Carries from CNS
5. **Effector**: Muscle/gland response

## Types of Reflexes

### Monosynaptic (Stretch Reflex)
- Example: Knee-jerk reflex
- Single synapse in CNS

### Polysynaptic
- Example: Withdrawal reflex
- Multiple interneurons

### Autonomic
- Heart rate regulation
- Pupillary reflex
- Digestion

## Examples

### Patellar Reflex
- Tap patellar tendon
- Quadriceps contracts
- Maintains posture

### Withdrawal Reflex
- Touch hot object
- Flexor muscles contract
- Extensor muscles relax
