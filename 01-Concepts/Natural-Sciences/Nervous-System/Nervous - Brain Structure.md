# Nervous - Brain Structure

> Detailed brain anatomy

## Key Concepts
1. Cerebrum
2. Cerebellum
3. Brain Stem
4. Limbic System
5. Ventricles

## Cerebrum

### Lobes
- **Frontal**: Planning, speech production, motor
- **Parietal**: Sensory processing, spatial
- **Temporal**: Memory, hearing, language comprehension
- **Occipital**: Vision
- **Insula**: Consciousness, emotion

### Structure
- Gray matter (outer): Cell bodies
- White matter (inner): Axons
- Corpus callosum: Connects hemispheres

## Cerebellum
- Coordination
- Balance
- Motor learning

## Brain Stem
- **Midbrain**: Visual/auditory reflexes
- **Pons**: Respiratory centers
- **Medulla**: Vital functions (heart rate, breathing)

## Limbic System
- **Hippocampus**: Memory formation
- **Amygdala**: Emotion
- **Hypothalamus**: Homeostasis

## Ventricles
- CSF production and circulation
- 4 ventricles system
