# Nervous - Action Potential

> Electrical signal in neurons

## Key Concepts
1. Resting Membrane Potential
2. Threshold
3. Depolarization
4. Repolarization
5. Refractory Period

## Resting Potential
- **Potential**: -70 mV
- **Maintained by**: Na+/K+ pump
- **Ion distribution**: More Na+ outside, more K+ inside

## Action Potential Phases

### 1. Resting State
- Voltage-gated channels closed
- Na+ channels ready

### 2. Depolarization
- Stimulus reaches threshold
- Na+ channels open
- Na+ rushes in
- Potential becomes positive

### 3. Repolarization
- Na+ channels close
- K+ channels open
- K+ rushes out
- Potential returns to negative

### 4. Refractory Period
- Absolute: Cannot fire again
- Relative: Higher threshold
