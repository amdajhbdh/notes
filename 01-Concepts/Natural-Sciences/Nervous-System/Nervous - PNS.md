# Nervous - PNS

> Peripheral nervous system

## Key Concepts
1. Somatic NS
2. Autonomic NS
3. Cranial Nerves
4. Spinal Nerves
5. Ganglion

## Divisions

### Somatic Nervous System
- Voluntary control
- Skeletal muscles
- Sensory input to CNS

### Autonomic Nervous System
- Involuntary
- **Sympathetic**: Fight or flight
- **Parasympathetic**: Rest and digest

## Nerves

### Cranial Nerves (12 pairs)
- I: Olfactory (smell)
- II: Optic (vision)
- III: Oculomotor
- VII: Facial
- X: Vagus
- ...etc

### Spinal Nerves (31 pairs)
- 8 cervical
- 12 thoracic
- 5 lumbar
- 5 sacral
- 1 coccygeal

## Autonomic Comparison

| Feature | Sympathetic | Parasympathetic |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Origin | T1-L2 | Cranial, S2-S4 |
| Neurotransmitter | Norepinephrine | Acetylcholine |
| Effect | Energy expenditure | Energy conservation |
