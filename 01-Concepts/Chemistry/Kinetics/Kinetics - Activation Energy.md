# Kinetics - Activation Energy

> Energy barrier in chemical reactions

## Key Concepts
1. Activation Energy ($E_a$)
2. Transition State Theory
3. Arrhenius Equation
4. Activation Complex
5. Temperature Dependence

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Arrhenius Equation**: $k = Ae^{-E_a/RT}$
- **Two-point form**: $\ln\frac{k_2}{k_1} = \frac{E_a}{R}\left(\frac{1}{T_1} - \frac{1}{T_2}\right)$
- **Activated Complex**: $E_a = E_{transition} - E_{reactants}$
