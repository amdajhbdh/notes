# Kinetics - Mechanisms

> Step-by-step pathways of chemical reactions

## Key Concepts
1. Reaction Mechanism
2. Elementary Steps
3. Molecularity
4. Rate-Determining Step
5. Steady-State Approximation

## Molecularity
- **Unimolecular**: Single molecule involved
- **Bimolecular**: Two molecules collide
- **Termolecular**: Three molecules (rare)

## Rate Laws from Mechanisms
- Slow step determines rate law
- Intermediate concentrations eliminated

## Example
$2NO + O_2 \rightarrow 2NO_2$

Mechanism:
1. $2NO \rightleftharpoons N_2O_2$ (fast equilibrium)
2. $N_2O_2 + O_2 \rightarrow 2NO_2$ (slow)

Rate law: $Rate = k[NO]^2[O_2]$
