# Solutions - Dilution

> Reducing concentration by adding solvent

## Key Concepts
1. Dilution Principle
2. Serial Dilution
3. Preparing Dilute Solutions
4. Safety Considerations

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Dilution Equation**: $C_1V_1 = C_2V_2$
- **Dilution Factor**: $DF = \frac{V_{final}}{V_{initial}}$

## Example
How much 6 M HCl needed for 500 mL of 0.5 M HCl?
$$6 \times V_1 = 0.5 \times 500$$
$$V_1 = 41.7 \text{ mL}$$
