# Solutions - Solubility

> Solubility and solubility equilibria

## Key Concepts
1. Solubility Definition
2. Saturated Solutions
3. Solubility Product ($K_{sp}$)
4. Common Ion Effect
5. Precipitation

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Solubility Product**: $K_{sp} = [A^+]^m[B^-]^n$
- **Molar Solubility**: $s = \sqrt[m+n]{K_{sp}/m^m n^n}$

## Example
$K_{sp}(AgCl) = 1.8 \times 10^{-10}$
$$s = \sqrt{1.8 \times 10^{-10}} = 1.3 \times 10^{-5} \text{ M}$$
