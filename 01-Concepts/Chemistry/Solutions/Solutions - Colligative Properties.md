# Solutions - Colligative Properties

> Properties depending on solute particle count

## Key Concepts
1. Vapor Pressure Lowering
2. Boiling Point Elevation
3. Freezing Point Depression
4. Osmosis
5. van't Hoff Factor

## Mathematical Formulas
- **Boiling Point Elevation**: $\Delta T_b = iK_b m$
- **Freezing Point Depression**: $\Delta T_f = iK_f m$
- **Osmotic Pressure**: $\Pi = iMRT$

## Example
Calculate freezing point of 0.5 m aqueous NaCl solution ($K_f = 1.86^\circ\text{C·kg/mol}$):
$$\Delta T_f = 2 \times 1.86 \times 0.5 = 1.86^\circ\text{C}$$
$$T_f = -1.86^\circ\text{C}$$
