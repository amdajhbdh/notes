## Mathematical Formulas
- **Molarity**: $M = \frac{n}{V}$ (mol/L)
- **Dilution**: $M_1V_1 = M_2V_2$

## Example
Calculate mass of NaCl needed for 500 mL of 0.5 M solution:
$$n = 0.5 \text{ M} \times 0.5 \text{ L} = 0.25 \text{ mol}$$
$$m = 0.25 \text{ mol} \times 58.44 \text{ g/mol} = 14.6 \text{ g}$$
