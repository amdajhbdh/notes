# Organic - Isomers

> Compounds with same molecular formula, different structure

## Key Concepts
1. Structural Isomers
2. Geometric Isomers (cis/trans)
3. Optical Isomers (enantiomers)
4. Diastereomers
5. Conformers

## Types of Isomerism

### Structural
- Chain isomers
- Position isomers
- Functional group isomers

### Stereoisomerism
- **Cis-Trans**: Restricted rotation (double bonds, rings)
- **Optical**: Chiral centers, non-superimposable mirror images

## Example
$C_4H_{10}$ has two isomers:
- n-butane: $CH_3-CH_2-CH_2-CH_3$
- isobutane: $(CH_3)_3CH$
