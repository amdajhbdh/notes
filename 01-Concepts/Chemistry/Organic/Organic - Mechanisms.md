## Mechanism Types

### $S_N1$ Mechanism
1. Leaving group departs (slow)
2. Nucleophile attacks (fast)
- Two-step process

### $S_N2$ Mechanism
1. Nucleophile attacks as leaving group leaves
- Concerted, single step
- Inversion of configuration

### Electrophilic Addition
1. $\pi$ bond attacks electrophile
2. Carbocation forms
3. Nucleophile attacks carbocation
