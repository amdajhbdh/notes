## Study Tips

### Active Learning
- Solve problems, don't just read
- Explain concepts out loud
- Draw diagrams for everything
- Link concepts across subjects

### Time Management
- 50 min study, 10 min break
- Switch subjects every 2 hours
- Review previous material daily

### Problem-Solving
1. Read carefully
2. Draw diagram
3. Identify knowns/unknowns
4. Solve step-by-step
5. Check answer
