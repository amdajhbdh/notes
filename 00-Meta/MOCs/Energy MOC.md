# Energy MOC

## Overview
Welcome to the Energy map of content. This covers work, energy, power, and energy conservation.

## Topics

### Energy Fundamentals
- Kinetic energy: $E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$
- Potential energy: $E_p = mgh$
- Mechanical energy: $E = E_k + E_p$

### Work and Power
- Work: $W = F \cdot d \cdot \cos(\theta)$
- Power: $P = \frac{W}{t} = F \cdot v$

### Energy Conservation
- Conservation of mechanical energy
- Energy transformations
- Efficiency

### Thermodynamics
- Heat and temperature
- Specific heat capacity
- Latent heat
