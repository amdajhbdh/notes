# 💬 Smart Chat

> [!info] AI-Powered Vault Assistant
> Chat with your BAC study vault using Ollama AI.

---

## 🎯 What is Smart Chat?

Smart Chat lets you ask questions about your notes and get AI-powered answers based on your vault content.

**Powered by:**
- Ollama (ministral-3:14b-cloud)
- Vector database (semantic search)
- Your 920+ notes

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

> [!example] How to Use
> 
> 1. Open command palette (`Ctrl/Cmd + P`)
> 2. Type "Smart Connections: Open Chat"
> 3. Ask your question
> 4. Get AI answer with sources

---

## 💡 Example Questions

> [!tip] Try These
> 
> ### Study Planning
> ```
> "What topics should I study this week?"
> "Create a study plan for matrices"
> "What are my weak areas in physics?"
> ```
> 
> ### Concept Explanation
> ```
> "Explain complex numbers in simple terms"
> "What's the relationship between force and acceleration?"
> "How do chemical reactions work?"
> ```
> 
> ### Content Discovery
> ```
> "Find all notes about electricity"
> "What exercises do I have on organic chemistry?"
> "Show me all BAC papers from 2010-2012"
> ```
> 
> ### Review Help
> ```
> "Summarize my physics notes"
> "What are the key formulas in calculus?"
> "List all important chemistry concepts"
> ```
