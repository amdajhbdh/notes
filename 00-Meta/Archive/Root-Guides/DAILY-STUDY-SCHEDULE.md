## 💡 STUDY TIPS

### Maximize Learning
1. **Active recall:** Test yourself, don't just read
2. **Spaced repetition:** Review at increasing intervals
3. **Interleaving:** Mix different topics
4. **Elaboration:** Explain concepts in your own words
5. **Concrete examples:** Use real exam problems

### Manage Energy
1. **Study in blocks:** 45-50 min focused work + 10 min break
2. **Vary topics:** Don't study same topic >2 hours
3. **Physical activity:** 30 min exercise daily
4. **Sleep:** 7-8 hours per night
5. **Nutrition:** Eat well, stay hydrated

### Track Progress
1. **Daily:** Log problems solved and accuracy
2. **Weekly:** Review progress and adjust
3. **Monthly:** Take full mock exams
4. **Identify:** Weak areas and focus there
