## ⚡ PHYSICS MASTERY PLAN

### Module 1: Electromagnetism & Induction (40% of physics grade)

**Topics to Master:**
1. Magnetic field calculations
2. Magnetic flux
3. Faraday's law of induction
4. Lenz's law
5. Self-inductance
6. Mutual inductance
7. Coil behavior

**Key Formulas:**
- B = $\mu_0$nI (solenoid field)
- Φ = BA cos(θ) (magnetic flux)
- ε = -dΦ/dt (Faraday's law)
- L = $\mu_0$N²A/l (self-inductance)

---

### Module 2: AC Circuits (30% of physics grade)

**Topics to Master:**
1. RLC circuits
2. Impedance calculations
3. Resonance frequency
4. Phase relationships
5. Power factor
6. Phasor diagrams

**Key Concepts:**
- Resonance occurs when XL = XC
- At resonance: Z = R (minimum)
- Phase angle: tan(φ) = (XL - XC)/R
