# Dynamics - Practice Easy

## Problem 1: Basic F = ma
A 5 kg object experiences a net force of 20 N. What is its acceleration?

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

F = ma
a = F/m = 20/5 = 4 m/s**2
</details>

---

## Problem 2: Weight Calculation
What is the weight of a 10 kg object on Earth? (g = 10 m/s**2)

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

W = mg = 10 * 10 = 100 N
</details>

---

## Problem 3: Net Force
Two forces act on an object: 30 N east and 40 N north. Find the net force.

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

Using Pythagorean theorem:
F_net = √(30**2 + 40**2) = √(900 + 1600) = √2500 = 50 N

Direction: tan(θ) = 40/30
θ = 53.1° north of east
</details>

---

## Problem 4: Acceleration from Rest
A car accelerates from rest at 3 m/s**2 for 5 seconds. What is its final velocity?

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

v = u + at
v = 0 + 3(5) = 15 m/s
</details>

---

## Problem 5: Friction Force
A 20 kg box on a surface with μ = 0.3. What is the friction force?

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

Normal force: N = mg = 20 * 10 = 200 N
Friction: f = μN = 0.3 * 200 = 60 N
</details>

---

## Problem 6: Two Forces
A 4 kg object has two horizontal forces: 15 N right and 7 N left. Find acceleration.

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

Net force: F_net = 15 - 7 = 8 N (right)
a = F/m = 8/4 = 2 m/s**2 (right)
</details>

---

## Problem 7: Stopping Distance
A 1000 kg car moving at 20 m/s brakes with force 5000 N. How long to stop?

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

a = F/m = -5000/1000 = -5 m/s**2
v = u + at
0 = 20 + (-5)t
t = 4 seconds
</details>

---

## Problem 8: Inclined Plane
A 10 kg object on a 30° incline. What is the parallel component of weight?

<details>
<summary>Solution</summary>

F_parallel = mg sin(θ)
= 10 * 10 * sin(30°)
= 100 * 0.5
= 50 N (down the incline)
</details>
