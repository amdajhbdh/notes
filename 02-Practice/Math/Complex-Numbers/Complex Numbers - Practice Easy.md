## Problems

### Basic Operations (1-8)

**1.** Calculate: $(3 + 2i) + (1 + 4i)$

**2.** Calculate: $(5 - 3i) - (2 - i)$

**3.** What is $\text{Re}(7 - 5i)$ and $\text{Im}(7 - 5i)$?

**4.** Find the conjugate of $4 + 3i$

**5.** Calculate: $2(3 + i)$

**6.** Multiply: $(2 + i)(2 - i)$

**7.** Calculate: $i**3$

**8.** Calculate: $i**8$

### Modulus (9-12)

**9.** Find $|3 + 4i|$

**10.** Find $|5 - 12i|$

**11.** Find $|6i|$

**12.** Find $|-2 + 2i|$

### Powers of i (13-16)

**13.** Calculate: $i**{10}$

**14.** Calculate: $i**{15}$

**15.** Calculate: $i**{20}$

**16.** Calculate: $i**{23}$

### Equality (17-20)

**17.** If $x + 3i = 5 + yi$, find $x$ and $y$

**18.** If $2x + 4yi = 8 + 12i$, find $x$ and $y$

**19.** Simplify: $(1 + i)**2$

**20.** Calculate: $(3 + 0i) + (0 + 2i)$

## Answers

<details>
<summary>Click to reveal answers</summary>

1. $4 + 6i$
2. $3 - 2i$
3. $\text{Re} = 7$, $\text{Im} = -5$
4. $4 - 3i$
5. $6 + 2i$
6. $5$ (note: $(a+bi)(a-bi) = a**2 + b**2$)
7. $-i$
8. $1$
9. $5$
10. $13$
11. $6$
12. $2\sqrt{2}$
13. $-1$ (since $i**{10} = i**{8} \cdot i**2 = 1 \cdot (-1)$)
14. $-i$ (since $i**{15} = i**{12} \cdot i**3 = 1 \cdot (-i)$)
15. $1$
16. $-i$ (since $i**{23} = i**{20} \cdot i**3 = 1 \cdot (-i)$)
17. $x = 5$, $y = 3$
18. $x = 4$, $y = 3$
19. $2i$ (expand: $1 + 2i + i**2 = 1 + 2i - 1$)
20. $3 + 2i$
